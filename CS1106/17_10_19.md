### List the titles of all the films made by the director of 'Vertigo'

`SELECT` title<br>
`FROM` movies `AS` m1<br>
`JOIN` movies `AS` m2<br>
`ON` m1.director = m2.director<br>
`WHERE` m1.title = 'Vertigo';
